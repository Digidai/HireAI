# Modern Hire - Deep Analysis

## Executive Summary

Modern Hire represents the convergence of hiring science, AI technology, and practical workflow automation into a comprehensive talent acquisition platform. Formed from the merger of Montage (video interviewing) and Shaker International (hiring assessments), Modern Hire combines validated assessment science with modern AI capabilities to deliver what the company calls "intelligent hiring experiences."

The platform's strength lies in its evidence-based approach: validated assessments, predictive analytics proven to forecast job performance, and AI scoring algorithms demonstrated to reduce hiring bias by over 70%. For organizations prioritizing both efficiency and quality in hiring—particularly those with high-volume needs or strong commitments to diversity and inclusion—Modern Hire offers a scientifically-grounded alternative to less rigorous recruiting technologies.

## Company Background and Evolution

### Formation and History

**Original Companies**:
- **Montage**: Founded 2012, pioneering video interviewing platform
- **Shaker International**: Established assessment and hiring science company
- **Merger**: Combined forces to create Modern Hire, integrating interviewing technology with assessment science

**Strategic Vision**:
The merger created a unique platform combining:
- Technology innovation from Montage's video interviewing platform
- Validated assessment science from Shaker's decades of hiring research
- AI and predictive analytics to enhance both interviewing and assessment

**Market Position**:
- Established presence in enterprise hiring technology market
- Strong reputation in regulated industries requiring validated hiring tools
- 90% user satisfaction rating based on verified customer reviews
- Emphasis on "hiring science" differentiates from purely technology-focused competitors

### "Hiring Science" Philosophy

Modern Hire's approach is grounded in industrial-organizational (I/O) psychology and data science:

**Core Principles**:
1. **Validation**: All assessments and predictive models validated against actual job performance
2. **Structure**: Standardized processes reduce variability and bias
3. **Prediction**: Focus on tools that forecast future performance, not just screen credentials
4. **Evidence**: Decisions based on data and research, not intuition or tradition
5. **Fairness**: Commitment to reducing bias and increasing diversity through proven methods

This science-first approach appeals particularly to:
- Regulated industries (healthcare, finance) requiring validated hiring tools
- Large enterprises emphasizing evidence-based talent decisions
- Organizations with strong DEI commitments seeking measurable bias reduction

## Platform Architecture and Capabilities

### Integrated Platform Components

Modern Hire's platform integrates three core capabilities:

1. **Virtual Interviewing Technology**
2. **Pre-Hire Assessments**
3. **Predictive Analytics and AI**

#### 1. Virtual Interviewing Platform

**Live Video Interviews**:
- Real-time video conversations with integrated collaboration tools
- Structured interview guides ensuring consistency across interviewers
- Note-taking and scoring features for collaborative evaluation
- Recording capabilities for review and compliance

**On-Demand Video Interviews**:
- Pre-recorded candidate responses to standardized questions
- Candidates complete on their schedule, eliminating coordination challenges
- Consistent questions ensure equitable evaluation across all candidates
- Efficient screening of large candidate volumes simultaneously

**Key Features**:
- **Candidate Self-Scheduling**: Automated calendar integration reduces administrative burden
- **Mobile-Optimized**: Candidates can interview from any device
- **Structured Templates**: Ensures compliance with best practices and legal requirements
- **Collaborative Evaluation**: Multiple stakeholders can review and score interviews
- **Automated Workflows**: Seamless progression from interview to next hiring stage

**Innovation**: Combines convenience of asynchronous interviewing with structure that reduces bias compared to unstructured phone or video screens.

#### 2. Pre-Hire Assessment Suite

**Virtual Job Tryout (VJT)**:
- Simulation-based assessments providing realistic job preview
- Candidates experience actual job tasks and scenarios
- Dual benefit: Assesses fit while giving candidates realistic expectations
- Proven to reduce turnover by setting accurate job expectations

**Chatbot Assessment Tool**:
- Conversational AI interface for candidate evaluation
- Natural language interaction feels more engaging than traditional tests
- Evaluates skills, work style, personality fit, and motivation
- Automated scoring using NLP algorithms

**Traditional Assessments**:
- Cognitive ability tests
- Personality and behavioral assessments
- Situational judgment tests
- Skills and knowledge evaluations

**Scientific Validation**:
- All assessments validated against job performance outcomes
- Predictive validity studies demonstrate forecast accuracy
- Adverse impact analyses ensure fairness across demographic groups
- Ongoing monitoring and refinement based on hiring outcomes

**Innovation**: Virtual Job Tryout approach provides both assessment and realistic job preview, improving both hiring quality and candidate self-selection.

#### 3. AI-Powered Scoring and Analytics

**Automated Scoring**:
- Natural language processing evaluates open-ended responses
- Structured rubrics ensure consistency
- Eliminates scorer bias and variability
- Provides immediate results without manual review

**Predictive Analytics**:
- Models forecast job performance, retention, and other outcomes
- Based on validation studies linking assessment results to success metrics
- Continuously improved as more hiring outcome data accumulates
- Risk scoring identifies candidates likely to underperform or leave early

**Bias Reduction**:
- Proven 70%+ reduction in hiring bias through structured processes
- Blind review options remove demographic information
- Standardized evaluation criteria applied consistently
- Algorithms audited for adverse impact across protected groups

**Reporting and Insights**:
- Dashboard analytics on hiring funnel performance
- Quality of hire tracking over time
- Diversity metrics and adverse impact monitoring
- Time-to-fill and efficiency analytics

## Implementation and Best Practices

### Getting Started

**Implementation Process**:
1. **Job Analysis**: Define competencies and success criteria for each role
2. **Assessment Selection**: Choose validated assessments appropriate for role requirements
3. **Interview Structure**: Develop structured interview guides
4. **Platform Configuration**: Set up workflows, scoring rubrics, and integrations
5. **User Training**: Train hiring teams on platform use and best practices
6. **Pilot and Refine**: Test with subset of roles, gather feedback, optimize

**Success Factors**:
- Executive sponsorship for structured, science-based approach
- Commitment to using data-driven insights vs. gut feel
- Adequate time for implementation and hiring team training
- Clear communication with candidates about assessment purpose
- Ongoing monitoring of results and continuous improvement

**Advantages**:
- Stronger emphasis on validated science vs. pure AI automation

**Considerations**:
- Less emphasis on autonomous agent capabilities
- May not offer same breadth of sourcing and engagement automation
- More focused on screening/selection vs. end-to-end recruiting

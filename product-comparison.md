    <section class="section">
        <h2>üéØ Decision Framework: Which Platform Is Right for You?</h2>

        <div class="decision-cards">
            <div class="decision-card">
                <h3>If you're hiring 1,000+ employees/year...</h3>
                <p><strong>Consider:</strong> Workday Recruiter Agent, Eightfold.ai, HireVue</p>
                <p><strong>Why:</strong> Enterprise-grade platforms with proven scalability, advanced AI, and comprehensive features justify the investment at this volume.</p>
            </div>

            <div class="decision-card">
                <h3>If you're hiring 100-500 employees/year...</h3>
                <p><strong>Consider:</strong> Paradox, Greenhouse + integrations, SmartRecruiters</p>
                <p><strong>Why:</strong> Mid-market solutions with strong automation, good ROI, and manageable implementation complexity.</p>
            </div>

            <div class="decision-card">
                <h3>If you're hiring 20-100 employees/year...</h3>
                <p><strong>Consider:</strong> Metaview, Ashby, Lever, Dover</p>
                <p><strong>Why:</strong> Point solutions and modern ATS platforms with lower entry costs and faster time-to-value.</p>
            </div>

            <div class="decision-card">
                <h3>If you're primarily hiring hourly/frontline workers...</h3>
                <p><strong>Consider:</strong> Paradox, Fountain, Workstream</p>
                <p><strong>Why:</strong> Mobile-first experiences, conversational interfaces, and high-volume automation optimized for this candidate demographic.</p>
            </div>

            <div class="decision-card">
                <h3>If you're primarily hiring technical talent...</h3>
                <p><strong>Consider:</strong> Dover, Gem, HackerRank (with ATS integration)</p>
                <p><strong>Why:</strong> Technical sourcing capabilities, developer-friendly interfaces, and integration with coding assessment platforms.</p>
            </div>
        </div>
    </section>

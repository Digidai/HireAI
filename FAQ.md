<section class="section">
<div class="section-header">
    <h2 class="section-title">About the Project</h2>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>What is HireAI?</h3>
<p>HireAI is a comprehensive collection of HR AI products and solutions. We track and organize various AI tools used in the Human Resources field, providing information and selection guides for HR professionals.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Who maintains this project?</h3>
<p>This project is maintained by contributors from the HR and AI communities. It is an open-source project hosted on GitHub, and we welcome contributions from anyone interested in HR technology.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>How is the content organized?</h3>
<p>Our content is organized based on Josh Bersin's five-stage technological evolution framework:</p>
<ul>
<li>1990s - Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS)</li>
<li>2000s - Candidate Marketing & Assessment</li>
<li>2010s - Onboarding/Workflow/Integrated Sourcing</li>
<li>2020s - Intelligent Assessment, Diversity, Career</li>
<li>2024+ - Agentic AI Platforms</li>
</ul>
</div>
</section>

<section class="section">
<div class="section-header">
    <h2 class="section-title">Content and Updates</h2>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>How often is the content updated?</h3>
<p>We aim to update the collection regularly as new HR AI products emerge and existing ones evolve. Major updates typically occur monthly, but we also make smaller updates as needed.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>How can I contribute to this project?</h3>
<p>Contributions are welcome! You can:</p>
<ul>
<li>Add new HR AI products</li>
<li>Update information about existing products</li>
<li>Improve documentation</li>
<li>Translate content to other languages</li>
<li>Report issues or suggest improvements</li>
</ul>
<p>Please see our <a href="https://github.com/Digidai/HireAI/blob/master/CONTRIBUTING.md">Contributing Guidelines</a> for more details.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>How do you ensure the accuracy of the information?</h3>
<p>We strive to maintain accurate and up-to-date information by:</p>
<ul>
<li>Regularly reviewing product websites and documentation</li>
<li>Following industry news and announcements</li>
<li>Accepting contributions from the community</li>
<li>Verifying information before merging changes</li>
</ul>
<p>If you find any inaccurate information, please let us know by opening an issue on GitHub.</p>
</div>
</section>

<section class="section">
<div class="section-header">
    <h2 class="section-title">Using the Collection</h2>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>How can I use this collection?</h3>
<p>This collection can help you:</p>
<ul>
<li>Research and compare HR AI products</li>
<li>Stay informed about trends in HR technology</li>
<li>Make informed decisions when selecting HR tools</li>
<li>Understand the evolution of HR technology</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Can I use this information commercially?</h3>
<p>Yes, the information in this collection can be used commercially. The project is licensed under the MIT License, which allows for commercial use, modification, distribution, and patent use.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Do you provide product recommendations?</h3>
<p>We aim to provide objective information about HR AI products rather than specific recommendations. However, our analysis may highlight strengths and weaknesses of different products to help you make informed decisions.</p>
</div>
</section>

## About AI in HR Technology

Artificial Intelligence has revolutionized HR technology, enabling capabilities that were impossible just a decade ago. From resume parsing to predictive analytics, AI powers the next generation of talent acquisition and management tools.

### Key AI Applications in HR

| Application | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Resume Screening** | Automated parsing and ranking of candidate applications |
| **Candidate Matching** | AI-powered job-to-candidate matching algorithms |
| **Predictive Analytics** | Forecasting hiring outcomes and employee performance |
| **Conversational AI** | Chatbots and virtual assistants for candidate engagement |
| **Bias Detection** | Identifying and mitigating hiring biases |

### Evaluation Considerations

When evaluating AI-powered HR products, consider:
- **Transparency**: How explainable are the AI decisions?
- **Bias Controls**: What safeguards exist against algorithmic bias?
- **Data Privacy**: How is candidate data used and protected?
- **Integration**: How does the AI connect with your existing systems?

## About Video Interviewing

Video interviewing has become essential for modern recruiting, enabling remote assessment while saving time and travel costs. Solutions range from simple video calls to AI-powered analysis platforms.

### Video Interview Types

| Type | Description | Best For |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Live** | Real-time video conversation | Final rounds, executive roles |
| **On-Demand** | Pre-recorded candidate responses | High-volume screening |
| **AI-Analyzed** | Automated assessment of responses | Structured evaluation |

### Platform Considerations

- **Integration**: Connect with your ATS and calendar
- **Candidate Experience**: Easy-to-use interface, mobile support
- **Recording**: Secure storage and sharing of interviews
- **AI Features**: Transcription, sentiment analysis, scoring
- **Compliance**: GDPR/CCPA-ready with consent management

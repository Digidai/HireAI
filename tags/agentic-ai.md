## About Agentic AI in HR

Agentic AI represents the latest evolution in HR technology. Unlike traditional AI that provides recommendations, agentic systems can autonomously execute complex workflowsâ€”scheduling interviews, sending personalized outreach, and even making preliminary screening decisions.

### Agentic vs. Traditional AI

| Aspect | Traditional AI | Agentic AI |
|--------|---------------|------------|
| **Role** | Advisor/Recommender | Autonomous Actor |
| **Actions** | Suggests next steps | Executes workflows |
| **Oversight** | Human makes decisions | Human sets guardrails |
| **Complexity** | Single-task focused | Multi-step orchestration |

### Key Considerations

- **Governance**: Define clear boundaries for autonomous actions
- **Audit Trails**: Comprehensive logging for compliance
- **Fallback**: Human escalation paths when AI is uncertain
- **Testing**: Extensive validation before production deployment

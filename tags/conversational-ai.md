## About Conversational AI in Recruiting

Conversational AI transforms candidate engagement through natural language interfaces. From answering FAQs to scheduling interviews, these tools provide 24/7 support while freeing recruiters for high-value interactions.

### Conversational AI Capabilities

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **FAQ Handling** | Answer common questions instantly |
| **Screening** | Collect qualification data conversationally |
| **Scheduling** | Book interviews without human intervention |
| **Nurturing** | Keep candidates engaged throughout the process |
| **Multilingual** | Support candidates in their preferred language |

### Implementation Tips

- Train on your actual candidate questions
- Define clear escalation triggers to human recruiters
- Monitor conversation quality and sentiment
- Ensure accessibility and mobile optimization
- Comply with disclosure requirements (bot identification)

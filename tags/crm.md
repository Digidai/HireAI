## About Candidate Relationship Management

Recruitment CRM (Candidate Relationship Management) helps organizations build relationships with potential candidates before they apply. Like sales CRM, it focuses on pipeline development and nurturing.

### CRM vs. ATS

| Aspect | CRM | ATS |
|--------|-----|-----|
| **Focus** | Pre-applicant engagement | Post-application tracking |
| **Contacts** | Passive candidates, prospects | Active applicants |
| **Goal** | Build relationships | Process applications |
| **Metrics** | Engagement, pipeline growth | Time-to-fill, conversion |

### Key CRM Features

- **Talent Pools**: Segment and organize candidates by skills, interests
- **Email Campaigns**: Nurture sequences for different audiences
- **Event Management**: Track career fair and recruiting event leads
- **Landing Pages**: Capture interest before job openings exist
- **Pipeline Analytics**: Measure engagement and conversion

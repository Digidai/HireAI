## Overview

Metaview is revolutionizing the interview process by eliminating one of recruiting's most time-consuming tasks: taking notes. Using advanced AI, Metaview generates comprehensive, high-quality interview notes automatically, allowing recruiters and hiring managers to focus entirely on the candidate conversation.

The platform's tagline—"AI-generated, superhuman-quality interview notes"—isn't hyperbole. Early customers report that Metaview saves them approximately **one full working day per week**, time previously spent writing up interview notes and summaries.

## The Problem Metaview Solves

Traditional interviewing creates a fundamental tension:

- **Focus on the candidate** → Miss important details, take incomplete notes
- **Focus on note-taking** → Miss conversational cues, seem distracted, damage candidate experience

This "note-taking paradox" has plagued recruiters for decades. Even experienced interviewers struggle to:
- Capture exact quotes and specific examples
- Document body language and tone
- Remember details from back-to-back interviews
- Write up comprehensive notes after each conversation

The result? Inconsistent documentation, decision-making based on incomplete information, and countless hours spent on administrative work instead of strategic talent acquisition.

## How Metaview Works

### 1. **Automatic Recording & Transcription**

Metaview integrates with major video conferencing platforms:
- Zoom
- Microsoft Teams
- Google Meet
- Phone calls (with dial-in)

When you join an interview, Metaview:
- Automatically joins the call
- Records the audio/video (with proper consent)
- Transcribes the conversation in real-time
- Identifies speakers and timestamps key moments

### 2. **AI-Powered Note Generation**

Here's where the magic happens. Metaview's AI doesn't just transcribe—it **understands**:

- **Structure Recognition**: Identifies question-answer patterns, technical discussions, behavioral examples
- **Key Information Extraction**: Pulls out skills mentioned, experience details, motivations, concerns
- **Smart Summarization**: Creates concise yet comprehensive notes that capture the essence of each discussion
- **Template Customization**: Adapts to your company's specific interview rubrics and evaluation criteria

The resulting notes are:
- Well-organized and easy to scan
- Comprehensive enough for thorough evaluation
- Consistent across different interviewers
- Immediately available after the interview ends

### 3. **Insights & Analytics**

Beyond note-taking, Metaview provides valuable intelligence:

- **Highlight Reels**: Automatically marks moments of particular interest (red flags, strong answers, technical discussions)
- **Talk Time Analysis**: Shows interviewer vs. candidate speaking ratios
- **Question Tracking**: Monitors which questions get asked consistently vs. skipped
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Detects candidate enthusiasm and engagement levels

### 4. **Seamless Integration**

Metaview connects with your existing recruiting tech stack:

**ATS Integration**:
- Greenhouse
- Lever
- Ashby
- Workable
- And 15+ others

Notes automatically sync to candidate profiles, ensuring everyone on the hiring team has access to complete information.

## Common Questions

**Q: Do candidates react negatively to being recorded?**
A: Most candidates are neutral or positive once they understand the benefits (more accurate evaluation, less distracted interviewer). Opt-out rates are typically under 5%.

**Q: What if the audio quality is poor?**
A: Metaview works best with good audio. For phone screens or poor connections, transcription accuracy may decrease, though the AI can often infer meaning from context.

**Q: Can I edit the AI-generated notes?**
A: Yes! Notes are fully editable. Most users find they need only minor adjustments, if any.

**Q: Is this legal in my jurisdiction?**
A: Recording laws vary by location. Metaview provides guidance and consent workflows, but you should consult legal counsel for your specific situation.
